Using GPS enabled devices

If the GISViewer is being used on a device that has an integrated GPS unit available and a suitable web browser, functionality to display the device's current location on the map can be activated. To find your current location using your device's GPS and zoom to that location one the map:


  1. Tap to turn GPS location on.

  2. Select OK on the permission pop-up.
    Map will pan & zoom to your location.

    .

  3. Tap to turn GPS location off.
