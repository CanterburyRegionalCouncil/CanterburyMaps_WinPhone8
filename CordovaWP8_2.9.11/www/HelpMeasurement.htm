Measuring

Interactively measure a distance or area in metric or imperial units.
Use the point location option to identify its X and Y coordinates (NZTM).



Follow the below steps to use the Measuring Tool:
  1. Click/tap the Area Distance or Location icon.
  2. Choose your desired measurement unit from the drop down list.
  3. Draw the area, line or point to measure on the map.
    Click/tap to place each point, double click/tap to finish drawing an area or line.
    Measurement values are shown under the Measurement Result header in the side menu.
  4. Use the point location option to capture a location's X and Y coordinates in NZTM.
