Drawing Tools

Draw and add text to your map using a range of predefined symbols and styles.



Follow the below steps to use the Drawing Tools:
  1. Click/tap a drawing icon and then one of its symbol settings styles.

    For Text click/tap and choose a font, colour, size & style.
  2. With a symbol settings style highlighted in the side menu, draw your selected graphic on the map.
    Note: to pan the map with the Drawing Tools open, click/tap the highlighted symbol settings style to toggle drawing off.
    Toggle drawing on by re-selecting a symbol settings style.

    • For point line or polygon click/tap to place vertices, double click/tap to finish drawing.
    • For freehand line freehand polygon circle ellipse or rectangle
      click/tap, drag & release to create your graphic.
    • For Text click/tap the map location to label, enter text into the Enter Label Text window then click/tap Add Label.
  3. To stop drawing click/tap .
  4. To remove all drawn graphics from the map click/tap .
